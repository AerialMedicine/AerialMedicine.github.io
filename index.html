ABOUT US

Thousands of Canadians are unable to receive urgent medical treatment due to distance, disability, self-isolation or simply relying on a poor delivery service.

We are a team of aspiring developers on a mission to create a fast and efficient medicine delivery system across Canada. At AerialMedicine, we manufacture our own aviation-grade drone technology in addition to providing quality delivery service.

THE DRONE

  • Fast

    Although our drones aren't as nimble as DRL racing drones, they are much faster, with a top speed of 300kph

  • Secure

    All of our drones are equiped with a pinpad to ensure the safe delivery of every package to it's correct client.

  • Capacity

    Our drones come with several seperate compartments so that each drone can make several deliveries in one trip if necessary.

  • Efficient

    Along with the built in solar pannelsour drones are completely electric, meaning that they produce 0 emissions.

  • Range

    With a range of up ro 500km, our drones are more than well equiped for long trips to deliver multiple packages.

  • Easy

    Our drones come with charging pods, that store the drone while it isn't being used. In these pods, the drones are easily accessible by the poeple who need to load them, but are still ready to go at a momments notice.
